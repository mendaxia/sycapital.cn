About Us

Songyuan Capital (‘SY Capital’for short) is a professional venture capital company that dedicated to find renewed impetus to the life style and wellbeing in China market. We focus on the growth opportunities of service consumption industry, by keeping eyes on new business model which derived from the evolution and intergenerational succession of cultural filed, and return to the real scene. We are interested in the early stage value with the theme of "lifestyle upgrade", including cultural, educational, entertainment, consumer, media and technology. Combined with rich investment experience and diverse value-added services of the post-investment management, we make the "multi-win" returns for investors, entrepreneurs and business ecosystem, meanwhile increasing the happiness of age.

According to Chinese culture,, “Song”(S) symbolizes robust and evergreen, while “Yuan”(Y) means the origins of things. SY’s mission is to roundly “fertilize” those companies in initial stage, and help them grow up like pine trees and built to last.

We have full heart of respect to both the entrepreneurship and investment. We deeply understand that entrepreneurship needs not only experience accumulation and wonderful ideas, but also execution and resource integration. SY Capital’s target is to optimize the allocation of resources: to discover, support and cultivate those real talented entrepreneurs, provide financial assistants and valuable services they need, and become a reliable partner all the way to their success.
